STEVE STRONTIUM

FACTS ABOUT STRONTIUM

  • Atomic Number: 38
  • Symbol: Sr
  • Discovered: 1790
  • Strontium atoms have 38 Electrons and 38 Protons.
  • Strontium is a silvery metal, with a pale yellow tint.
  • When Strontium is burned it gives off a red flame.
  • It can generate electricity for space vehicles and remote weather stations.
