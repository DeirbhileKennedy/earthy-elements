FACTS ABOUT BARIUM

  • Atomic Number: 56
  • Symbol: Ba
  • Discovered: 1808
  • Barium atoms have 56 Electrons and 56 Protons.
  • This element is a soft silvery metal. It is quite reactive.
  • Barium is never found in nature because it reacts with oxygen.
  • Barium can be combained with other elemtents so it can be found in pieces of machinery.
