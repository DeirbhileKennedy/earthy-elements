FACTS ABOUT BERYLLIUM

  • Atomic Number: 4
  • Symbol: Be
  • Discovered: 1798
  • Beryllium atoms have 4 Electrons and 4 Protons.
  • Beryllium is a very lightweight metal nd is also very rare.
  • It is a strong, but brittle metal. It is silver-grey metallic in color.
  • It can also be found in satellites and spacecrafts.
