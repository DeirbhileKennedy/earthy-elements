MANNY MAGNESIUM

FACTS ABOUT MAGNESIUM

  • Atomic Number: 12
  • Symbol: Mg
  • Discovered: 1808
  • Magnesium atoms have 12 Electrons and 12 Protons.
  • It is a light metal with a Silver-White colour.
  • Magnesium burns with a bright white light.
  • Magnesium can be found in medicene and the chlorophyll in plants.
