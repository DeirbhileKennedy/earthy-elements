CALLY CALCIUM

FACTS ABOUT CALCIUM

  • Atomic Number: 20
  • Symbol: Ca
  • Discovered: 1808
  • Calcium atoms have 20 Electrons and 20 Protons.
  • It is a soft, shiny-silvery metal.
  • Calcium burns with a bright orange-red flame.
  • Calcium can be found in bones and teeth which is super important for strength.
